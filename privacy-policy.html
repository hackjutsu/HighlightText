Privacy Policy for Text Highlighter

Last Updated: [Current Date]

1. Introduction

This privacy policy explains how Text Highlighter ("we", "our", or "the extension") collects, uses, and protects your information.

2. Information We Collect

The extension collects and stores the following types of data:

  • Highlights Data: Text selections you highlight and their colors are stored locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API. This data never leaves your device.
  • Error Tracking Data (Optional): With your explicit consent, we collect:
    • Error messages (sanitized to remove personal information)
    • Extension version
    • Timestamp of errors

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Highlights Data: To save and restore your highlights across page reloads
  • Error Tracking Data: To improve the extension's stability and fix bugs

4. Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage API:

  • Highlights are stored per webpage URL
  • Error logs are automatically deleted after 30 days
  • A maximum of 50 error logs are kept at any time

5. Data Sharing

We do not share any of your data with third parties. All data remains on your local device.

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights:

  • Choose whether to enable error tracking during installation
  • Export your highlights as text files
  • Clear all stored data by uninstalling the extension
  • Export or clear error logs through the extension menu

7. Data Security

We implement security measures including:

  • Local-only storage of all data
  • Sanitization of error messages to remove sensitive information
  • Automatic data cleanup

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.
